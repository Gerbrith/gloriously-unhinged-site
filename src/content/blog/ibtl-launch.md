*In Between the Lies* by Savanna Rioux is officially out in the world, and we are losing our minds about it.

## The book

He was buried for three centuries. Hers was the blood that woke him.

A volatile ancient vampire. A stubborn woman who refuses to be afraid of him. A curse that binds them. A past that wants him back.

This is a dark paranormal romance about grief, devotion, and the kind of love that refuses to die. It has enemies-to-lovers, forced proximity, and a slow burn with bursts of chaos — because Savanna doesn't do anything by halves.
